How is completion calculated?

Projects are marked complete if their descriptions contain completion keywords like:

"completed", "finished", "done", "fixed", "implemented", "resolved", "delivered", "shipped", "closed", "merged", "deployed", "released", "solved", "accomplished", "achieved"

Projects with incomplete indicators ("todo", "pending", "in progress") are excluded even if they have completion keywords.
